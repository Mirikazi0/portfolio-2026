Symph

Redesigning a teacher platform's landing page to tell a clearer story, open B2B sales conversations, and prepare for global expansion.

Role

UI/UX Design Intern

Timeline

4 Months

Platform

Web

Tools

Figma, FigJam, Notion

The Challenge

Symph is a platform designed to help teachers create and manage lesson content more effectively. Despite having a genuinely powerful product, the existing landing page failed to clearly communicate who it was for, how it worked, or why schools should adopt it at an institutional level.

The team was preparing for B2B outreach to school administrators and needed a page that could do two things simultaneously: speak directly to individual teachers and initiate trust with decision-making buyers.

Process & Discovery

I began by auditing the existing page against a competitor set — looking at how EdTech B2B products like Canva for Education and Nearpod framed their value propositions. I then ran a lightweight content audit to identify every value claim the product made and whether there was evidence to back it up.

"The old page talked about features. Teachers care about outcomes — saving time on lesson prep, keeping students engaged. We needed to flip the frame."

Interviews with three active users confirmed the pattern: teachers were unclear on what Symph actually did in the first 5 seconds of landing on the page. The above-the-fold message was too abstract and product-focused.

The Solution

I restructured the entire narrative hierarchy — leading with a teacher-first outcome statement, followed by a demonstration of the product in context, and closing with a clear B2B value proposition aimed at administrators.

The new information architecture prioritized three persuasion layers: emotional resonance (I see myself using this), social proof (others like me trust it), and institutional clarity (this is safe for my school to adopt). Each section earned the next.

The Impact

The redesigned page significantly improved key engagement metrics and became the foundation for Symph's first B2B sales deck, directly enabling partnership conversations with school districts in the Philippines and Southeast Asia.
